Ashtanga yoga level 4

Syllabus

  • Philosophy
  • Relating the philosophy with its therapeutic insight to every day life of individuals & understanding the root cause of the ailmemts.
  • Anatomy
  • A detailed, in-depth study into the anatomy & physiology of human body.
  • Detailed study of the yoga therapy with respect to different ailments.
  • What not to teach for individuals suffering from different ailments.
  • Study of yoga for backache, nervous disorders, digestive disorders, arthritis & women's health.
  • Asana
  • Study & practice of asanas for treating different ailments
  • How to teach with the help of props(Guruji's technique).
  • Sequences for all the different disabilities & how to help one to overcome them.
  • Physiology
  • A brief study of the physiology of the human mind to help them move towards higher consciousness.
  • Practice of different meditational techniques to overcome one's own inhibitions & help them to achieve unaltered happiness.
  • Pranayama
  • Kapalbhati
  • Bhastrika Pranayama
  • Surya bhedhana pranayama
  • Nadi shodhana pranayama
  • Bhramari pranayama
  • In depth study of all the above pranayama Involves the theoretical study of each of the above & practice sequence too that can be followed for upto 18 months .
  • Complete study of chapter 2 of hatha yoga pradapika & study from "light on pranayama" , By B.K.S. Iyenagar
  • Chanting
  • Yoga shloka
  • Surya Namaskara shloka & 13 mantras
  • Shanti mantra
  • Pranayama shloka
  • The above will be taught to students in person & by the end of the ttc they will be able to chant fluently too so that a part of Sanskrit language, the of all languages stays in them for every.
  • Shatkarma (cleansing techniques )
  • Jala neti
  • Sutra neti
  • Shankapakshalana
  • Vamana dhouti
  • Duration and intake
  • Weekday : Every Tuesday , Thursday & Saturday - 2 months
  • Weekends : 3 months
  • A maximum of 10 students/ttc. Individual attention will be given all the students to bring out the best in them.
  • Pre requisites
  • Must have completed the first level of Ashtanga yoga/ Hatha yoga
  • Thesis presentation
  • At the end of the course students are advised to write a thesis on 3 ailments & present the paper along with pre, post data.
  • Students are required to present atleast one paper in any one of the medical journal too and they will be assisted for the same.
  • Books to be referred
  • Yoga therapy, B.K.S.Iyengar
  • Yoga therapy for different ailments, SVYASA
  • Yoga Anatomy
