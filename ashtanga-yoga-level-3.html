Ashtanga yoga level 3

Syllabus

  • Philosophy
  • The complete study of 1st & 2nd chapter of hatha yoga Pradipika
  • Introduction to mudras & Bhadhas as discussed in Hatha yoga pradipika
  • A study of interrelation of Bhagavad gita & yoga sutras.
  • Study of Hatha ratnavali & Gheranda Samhita
Shatkarma ( The six main cleansing techniques)
  • Kriyas
  • Jalaneti
    Sutra Neti
    Kaphalabati
    Trataka
    Shankprakshalana
    Dhouti
  • In addition to learning the technique, students will have the opportunity to gain personal experience in performing these kriyas during the course.
  • Anatomy
  • An introduction to anatomy & physiology of human body.
  • Detailed study of the yoga therapy with respect to different ailments.
  • What not to teach for individuals suffering from different ailments.
  • Asana
  • The beginners & intermediate level from The light on Yoga & the first few weeks of advance level for those who wish to push their limits.
  • Asanas as described in the Hatha yoga texts.
  • Sequencing of asanas as described in Hatha yoga.
  • Pranayama
  • Kapalbhati
  • Bhastrika Pranayama
  • Surya bhedhana pranayama
  • Nadi shodhana pranayama
  • Bhramari pranayama
  • In depth study of all the above pranayama Involves the theoretical study of each of the above & practice sequence too that can be followed for upto 18 months .
  • Complete study of chapter 2 of hatha yoga pradapika & study from "light on pranayama" , By B.K.S. Iyenagar
  • Meditation
  • There will regularly be short sessions of meditation (around 30-45minutes per session).
  • Chanting
  • Yoga shloka
  • Surya Namaskara shloka & 13 mantras
  • Shanti mantra
  • Pranayama shloka
  • The above will be taught to students in person & by the end of the ttc they will be able to chant fluently too so that a part of Sanskrit language, the of all languages stays in them for every.
  • Duration and intake
  • Weekday : Every Tuesday , Thursday & Saturday - 2 months
  • Weekends : 3 months
  • A maximum of 10 students/ttc. Individual attention will be given all the students to bring out the best in them.
  • Pre requisites
  • All are welcome
  • Presentation and yoga therapy
  • Students are required to prepare a mini Students are required to submit this for review and then present to the class their chosen condition and how to treat it with yoga.
  • Preparatory reading
  • Light on Yoga by B K S Iyengar
  • Yoga: Asanas Pranayama Mudras Kriyas by Vivekananda Kendra Prakashan
  • Hatha Yoga Pradipika by Swami Muktibodhananda (Bihar Yoga)
  • Asana Pranayama Mudra Bandha by Swami Satyananda Saraswati
  • Light on Pranayama , B.K.S.Iyengar
